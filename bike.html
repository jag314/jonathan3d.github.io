Bicycle of the future

The Spacelander bicycle was designed by Benjamin Bowden for the 1946 exhibition Britain Can Make It

Originally known as the Classic, the streamlined design was said to represent what the bicycle of the future was supposed to look like.

I took that concept and made my own interpretations of what the intention of the artist was, I found curiosity all over the shapes, it tries to materialize a collective idea of devices both beautiful and futuristic. How will be transportation in 50 years? That a marvelous question that have made to flourish all the designers and engineers imagination.
