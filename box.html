Nezuko Box

A handcrafted wood box for carrying a demon.

This handcreafted wood box was given to Kamado Tanjiro by his sensei Sakonji Urokodaki, it was buit with the best materials in order to protect what is inside; a demon , his sister.

When I watched Kimetsu No Yaiba for the first time I felt in love with the design of this box so when designing it, I made sure of using the correct dimensions and material. Go see the 3D model .
