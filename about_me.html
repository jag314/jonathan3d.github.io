I am deeply inspired by nature and it's organic shapes,
My goal is to bring ideas to real life using 3D print & Design
I love teaching and learnings.

I was born in Nicargua and then moved to Costa Rica when I was 12 years old. Since that my main goal has been to educate myself not just academically but spiriyually too .

I am currently studying Civil Engineering, I graduated as Project Management and Budgeting Technician, during this time I have taken educational courses about 3D Design, Organic Design, Design Thinking, UI Design and Programming.

I have been teaching 3D Print & Design to different national institutions as universities and nonprofits and also to international institutions like Tribu Maker; an online platform that provides educational courses to people from all Latin America, this has provided me with a wider point of view and understanding of communications and service. Being able to teach to people have made me develop a sense off awareness and gratefulness

I am running and enterprise called Inity, a 3D print and Design studio focused on providing service to a variety of clients, our mission is to give exceptional solutions using 3D design and creative thinking. .
