M A P A R A

What does it make you to feel comfortable?.

MaPara is the first piece of furniture of a collection I am working on, I am inspired on the irregular shape of plants and trees so the main purpose of this design is to bring that sensation of being around trees, breathing and contemplating the how majestic the forest is.

Everything is connected, so it does not matter if you are in a city far away from green space, you can have furniture that can make you remember and reconnect with your green space within. Go look the 3D Model
