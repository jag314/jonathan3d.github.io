Mano de Tigre (Monstera deliciosa)

There is a blue print already written, we just have to look around.

Mano de Tigre (Monstera deliciosa) is one of my favorite plants. The specie has holes on its leaf, I have always wondered why does it have that peculiar shape.

One day I was thinking about a lamp idea, then I remembered how this plant looks and thought: why is it so pleasant to look. Then I found it, I draw some holes around following sacred geometry and when I realized I was there just creating and changing all the purpose of the design.

This design represents the natural flow of the idea, how it expands, change and readapt, I 3D printed using glow in the dark filament so when the lights go off it looks like some sort of divine device, or at least that what I feel. Go check the 3D model yourself
