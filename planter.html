Concrete Planter

How to Design and 3D Print pot molds .

I have many plants at home and in some cases I feel like the beauty of the plant does not match the simplicity of the pot. That made me want to create my own pots, so I began my research and found out that there are many ways of casting concrete, plaster and many other materials for creating planter pots, but you will need to have a mold.

I designed the mold for my planter pot Idea and 3D printed it, the assembly is easy to use and 3D print. I learned so much with this project I am currently working on.
